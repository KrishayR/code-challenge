How the Code Challenge Works

  1. To play, login every day between April 5 and April 25 and answer the Code Challenge question.
  2. The first 21 questions are answered in Python code. If all 21 are answered correctly, players earn a $25 CodeWizardsHQ gift card and access to the Boss Level.
  3. In the Boss Level, help Nym defeat Glorm by writing a program in Python or JavaScript. If your program is successful, you will have a chance to win a $100 cash prize and CodeWizardsHQ t-shirt!
  4. First, second, and third place champions will be determined by public vote from May 3 through May 7 and a Wizard Panel grading from May 8 through 13. Winners announced on May 14.

Good luck!
